# ☕Bright Coffee Shop- Sales Analysis

# 📁 Description
This repository entails a sales performance analysis that was conducted for the new CEO to understand the performance of the business for the period of 6 months, i.e. from 1st January to 1st June 2023.

# 🎯Objective
The objective of this analysis is to derive actionable insights from historical data and develop a presentation that will support the new CEO with his mandate to fast track revenue growth and enhance product performance. The data should enable clear visualization of the following insights:
- Top‑performing products in terms of revenue generation
- Peak operating periods, highlighting the times of day when the store performs best
- Sales trends across product categories and time intervals
- Data‑driven recommendations to enhance overall sales performance

# 📊 Tools Used
- **Data processing**: The SQL queries was completed in *Databricks* platform, then dowloaded the processed data into Microsoft excel for analysis
- **Project Planning & Archetecture**: I used *Miro* to plan how to do this project, i used brainstorming and flow diagrams
- **Data Visualization**: To visualise i used *Microsoft Excel*, this where i built the analysis using pivot tables and charts
- **Presentation**: Final analysis was presented on *Microsoft Powerpoint*

# 💡Key insights
- Revenue Trend: Strong growth from January to June, rising from 81K to 166K (+104%).
- Product Mix: Coffee and Tea lead the business, contributing 70% of total revenue.
- Store Performance: All three stores deliver similar results, each generating roughly R230K–R236K.
- Customer Behaviour: Almost all customers (99.6%) fall into the low‑spend segment, spending under R10 per transaction.
- Low Performers: Packaged Chocolate and Regular Syrup show weak performance and need attention.
- Weekend Opportunity: Lower weekend traffic suggests room to grow sales during these periods.

# ✅Recommendations

- **Staffing Strategy**: Maintain full weekday staffing to support strong demand, streamline weekend staffing to match lower traffic, and introduce performance bonuses to incentivize employees who meet monthly sales targets.
- **Pricing adjustment**: Increase prices on high‑demand products like Coffee and Tea by 10–15%, bundle underperforming items to improve uptake, and reassess Packaged Chocolate—which generates only R8,408.80 in revenue—to determine whether it should be improved or discontinued.
- **Marketing Campaigns**:To stimulate demand during low‑traffic periods, introduce a late‑afternoon happy hour with modest price reductions, bundle underperforming products to improve their uptake, and use evening discounts—such as bakery coupons—to clear inventory while strengthening customer loyalty.
